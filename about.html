Nightbug

Who are we and what are we doing?

Nightbug is a collective of developers from all kinds of different backgrounds, different levels of skills, coming together to build nice projects!

Our members know a variety of languages and each one is a specialist in their field!

Want to learn something new? Members usually share their projects and experiences for others to learn from!

Nightbug is a group made of people from diverse backgrounds, no matter what race, gender or preferences you have, everyone is welcome!
